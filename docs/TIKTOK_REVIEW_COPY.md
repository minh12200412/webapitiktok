TanPhatETek Social Publisher is a public-facing social publishing platform for authorized business users, brands, agencies, creators, and client workspaces. Users connect TikTok accounts through Login Kit, prepare approved video content, upload content to TikTok draft/inbox flow with Content Posting API, optionally Direct Post with explicit consent, schedule posts in the platform, and review TikTok profile, stats, and public video performance. The app uses TikTok OAuth scopes only after user authorization and does not use Share Kit or Webhooks.
